Your Health, Our Mission
Intercare Hospital, a leading hospital in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, provides comprehensive medical care for all your needs. Our team of experienced specialists offers a wide range of services. We are committed to your health and well-being

Our story

Health is not just about what you're eating. It's also about what you're thinking and saying. Our clinic was established on October 19, 2010 with 100 employees, the purpose of which we set up is to help many people who want to treat disease, we treat people of all ages, whether old or young. We have received a lot of praise from the patients who come here for treatment. So far, the number of patients who have come here for treatment has reached 3,2000.

Treat children and the elderly in need to be healthy, reduce the number of deaths in Cambodia and increase the well-being of the people of Cambodia.
